## Summary

A brief trailer for Joe's Connection Course. Joe states that every human wants to accomplish something—whether a happy marriage, thriving business, becoming an awesome parent, or overcoming self-limitations. From coaching thousands of the world's most successful professionals, he's found that the one thing that helps people accomplish what they want is connection: connection with others and connection with yourself. When we feel connected, we reduce anxiety, attract like-minded people, and turn conflict into opportunity.
