## Summary

Brett and Joe discuss the business behind Art of Accomplishment — how Joe accidentally fell into coaching from venture capital, how the online courses came to be (sparked by COVID), and the philosophy behind pricing and accessibility. Joe explains that his core motivation is enjoyment and following the deeper call, not ego or saving the world.

The conversation dives into the tension between making transformational work accessible and maintaining energetic exchange. Joe describes the money model as ensuring sustainability for the team while offering a range of price points. He reflects on the risks of spiritual tools being corrupted or weaponized, his approach to intellectual property (ask permission to use novel tools), and the inevitable pattern of good teachings being turned into new moralities. His five-year vision: the work extending far beyond him, with him blissfully unaware.
