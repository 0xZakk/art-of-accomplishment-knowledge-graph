## Summary

Joe outlines four ways that cultivating connection drives business success: product development, intuition in meetings, team performance, and sales. Connection starts internally—a deep breath, paying attention to your body, being grateful—and doesn't require anyone else to participate.

He shares the story of a CEO with five $100M+ exits who attributed his success to deeply connecting with customers before building anything, essentially letting the product be designed by the connection rather than by his own ideas. Joe extends the principle to meetings (feeling when something's off), teams (the best teams always feel connected), and sales (which is really just listening to find problems you can solve).
