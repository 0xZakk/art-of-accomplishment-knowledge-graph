## Summary

Joe Hudson introduces his YouTube channel and the concept of "applied self-awareness." He explains that this means understanding what motivates you, what you're good at, and how to change what causes you pain — and then applying that understanding to practical domains like parenting, leadership, starting a business, or being an artist.

The ultimate result of applied self-awareness, Joe says, is learning to love yourself more, which naturally extends to loving others and the world more easily. The channel covers topics ranging from how to start a project to how to approach a transformational journey, deal with emotions, and overcome procrastination.

## Key Quotes

> "Applied self-awareness means understanding what motivates you, understanding what you're good at. It also means that you understand how to change the things about yourself that are causing you pain."

> "Applied means how does that relate to being a great parent or being a great CEO or starting a business or being an artist."

> "The end result of applied self-awareness besides accomplishing a whole bunch of great things in the world is that you learn to love yourself more, and as you learn to love yourself you love others more easily and you love the world more easily."
