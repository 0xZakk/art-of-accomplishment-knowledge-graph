title: "Presence & Authenticity"

Joe's teachings on the direct experience of "I am"—authentic presence—and what keeps us from it.

## Key Insight

The expansiveness of truly being ourselves can be terrifying to the mind. We develop protective mechanisms (like doubt) to avoid this direct contact with who we really are.
