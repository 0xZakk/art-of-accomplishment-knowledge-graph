title: "Emotions & Emotional Processing"
---

Joe's teachings on welcoming, processing, and learning from emotions rather than avoiding them.

## Key Insight

The mind tells us emotions are dangerous—that sadness is never-ending, anger will destroy everything, fear will make us incapable. But when we fully welcome emotions, they move through us and bring gifts: clarity, connection, capability.
