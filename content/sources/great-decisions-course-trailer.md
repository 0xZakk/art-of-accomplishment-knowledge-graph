## Summary

This short trailer for the Art of Accomplishment "Great Decisions" course presents the core premise: we make 10,000 choices daily that shape every aspect of our lives, yet most of us feel uncomfortable around decisions. Modern neurology shows that decisions are made in the emotional center of the brain — if that center were injured, it would take hours to decide where to have lunch. Our bad decisions come from fear of emotional consequences: rejection, shame, helplessness. When we stop being afraid of our emotions, we become clear and make great decisions naturally.
