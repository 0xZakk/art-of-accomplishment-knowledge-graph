## Summary

In this coaching session from a live Q&A, a participant asks how to create wealth and financial stability. Joe immediately asks them to list the bad things about money, revealing beliefs that money is dirty, guilty, and unworthy. Joe points out the contradiction: why would you want something you've labeled as terrible?

Joe guides the participant to feel the relationship they have with money—wanting it but judging it, craving it but thinking it's bad—and asks when they first felt that dynamic. The participant immediately identifies their mother. Joe then reframes money as "the sun"—abundant, neutral, shining on everyone, involved in everything we see. When the participant lets this reframing in, they feel expansion rather than constriction, and can articulate what they'd do with abundance: make art, create jobs, travel, and make an impact.
