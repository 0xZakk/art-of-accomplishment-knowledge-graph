Joe uses golf to make the point that understanding is not the same as changing. You can read every book on the swing and still not be able to play, because the skill lives in the body, not in the description of it. Insight is where most transformational work stops, and it's also where most transformation fails.

> "I can read a book on how to play golf, but until I actually start playing golf, it's not going to happen."

This is why head-based material — podcasts, videos, even this lesson — can only take someone so far. The intellect can fully grasp a change while the heart and nervous system stay exactly where they were. Real change requires running precise experiments and feeling the new way of being in the body, not just thinking about it. The deepest shifts come when emotions long tucked away get released and the nervous system actually moves.
