When you see the inner critic as an enemy, you're locked in a fight you can never win. But when you see it as a guide, it points to exactly the places in you that haven't been healed, accepted, or loved.

> "If you see it as a guide it's going to point to exactly the places in you that you haven't healed or that you don't accept or that you don't love."

Every topic the inner critic obsesses over—weight, productivity, worth, competence—is a signpost pointing toward unresolved pain. Rather than the voice being the problem, it's revealing where the real work is. This reframe transforms the inner critic from an obstacle into one of the most useful tools for personal growth.
