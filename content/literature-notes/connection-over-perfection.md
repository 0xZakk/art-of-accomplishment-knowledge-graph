People think they need to get it right or be perfect to accomplish something. Iteration is actually a much better approach—quicker, with better results. And the trick for great iteration is connection: stay connected with yourself, your audience, your customers, your team.

> "Oftentimes people think that they have to get it right or be perfect to accomplish something, which is really not the case. Iteration is a much better approach."

Connection + iteration beats perfection every time. Perfection isolates (you withdraw to get it right). Connection keeps the feedback loop alive so each iteration improves.
