When you're yourself, everything that says "yes" to you is saying yes to the real you.

> "If we are ourselves, then the things that say yes to us are the things that are saying yes to us, not the contorted version of ourselves."

## The Alternative

When you contort yourself to please:
- People who hire you want the contorted version
- People who like you like the mask
- You have to keep performing

When you're yourself:
- People who support you support *you*
- People who like you like who you actually are
- No performance required

## The Design Principle

Being yourself isn't just about integrity—it's about architecture. You're designing a life through your choices.

> "We actually design a life by being ourselves. We design a life of people who like us for us, of people who want to support us for us, of people who believe in us for who we are."

## The Promise

> "If you just be yourself, you'll realize there's nothing wrong with you. You'll realize that there is a whole world designed for you waiting for you to be you."

The world that fits you already exists. You just have to be yourself to find it.
